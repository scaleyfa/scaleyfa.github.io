About Us

Scaley Feather Adventures was started in 2008 by two best friends, being outdoorsman, it was the name of our boat. In the beginning, we worked with pallet wood, building things for friends and family. We began selling to recoup the cost of supplies and equipment, eventually, selling at art fairs and bazars. Now SFA has evolved into salvage, reclaimed, and barn wood projects.

We love starting with ugly unwanted wood, then revealing the beautiful art that God and mother nature has given us to see. With hard work and determination, we turn what was nothing into something.
